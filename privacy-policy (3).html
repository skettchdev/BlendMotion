BlendMotion

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Summary: BlendMotion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your camera tracking data stays on your local network and is never sent to any server.

1. Introduction

BlendMotion ("the App") is a camera tracking application developed by Skettch. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration required
  • No personal information collected
  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No cookies or identifiers
  • No data sent to external servers

3. Camera & Motion Data

The App uses your device's camera through Apple's ARKit framework solely for motion tracking purposes. This data:

  • Is processed entirely on your device
  • Is sent only to your local computer via Wi-Fi (UDP)
  • Is never stored on the device or any server
  • Is never transmitted over the internet
  • Contains only camera position and rotation — no images or video are captured or transmitted

4. Network Communication

BlendMotion communicates exclusively over your local Wi-Fi network using the UDP protocol. The data packets contain only numerical camera position and rotation values. No data leaves your local network.

5. Third-Party Services

BlendMotion does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain any ads.

6. Data Storage

BlendMotion does not store any data on your device beyond the app's basic settings (server IP address and port number), which are stored locally using standard iOS mechanisms.

7. Children's Privacy

BlendMotion does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
