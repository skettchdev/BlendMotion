Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's what we do — and don't do — with your data.

Effective date: January 1, 2026

Overview

BlendMotion is designed with privacy at its core. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

BlendMotion does not collect any of the following: personal information (name, email, etc.), device identifiers or analytics, usage or tracking data, location data, or camera/microphone recordings.

Network Communication

BlendMotion communicates exclusively over your local Wi-Fi network using UDP packets. Motion data is sent directly from your iPhone to the Blender addon running on your computer. No data leaves your local network.

Third-Party Services

BlendMotion does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

No user data is stored on-device or on any remote server by BlendMotion. The only data persisted locally is your connection settings (IP address and port), which remain on your device.

Children's Privacy

BlendMotion does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact
