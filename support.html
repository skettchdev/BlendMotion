Quick Start Guide

  1. Install the Blender Addon — Download the .zip file from Blender Market. In Blender, go to Edit → Preferences → Add-ons → Install and select the zip file.
  2. Enable the Addon — Search for "BlendMotion" in the addon list and check the box to enable it.
  3. Start the Server — Open the BlendMotion panel in the 3D viewport sidebar (N key) and click Start.
  4. Install the iPhone App — Download BlendMotion from the App Store.
  5. Connect — Make sure your iPhone and computer are on the same Wi-Fi network. Enter your computer's local IP address in the app and tap Connect.
  6. Track & Record — Move your iPhone to control the Blender camera. Tap Record to capture keyframes on the timeline.
💡 Tip: To find your computer's local IP, open a terminal and type ipconfig (Windows) or ifconfig (macOS/Linux). Look for the address starting with 192.168.
📷 Tip: Always hold your iPhone in landscape orientation (sideways) for the best tracking results. This matches Blender's camera orientation and gives you natural, cinematic motion.

Troubleshooting

Can't connect? Ensure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network. Check that your firewall isn't blocking the UDP port used by BlendMotion.

Laggy tracking? Close other apps on your iPhone and reduce the distance to your Wi-Fi router for a stronger signal.

Addon not showing up? Make sure you're running Blender 4.0 or later. Try restarting Blender after installation.
