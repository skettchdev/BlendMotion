FAQ

Common questions about BlendMotion.

Does BlendMotion require ARKit?
BlendMotion uses iOS Core Motion to track device orientation. ARKit is not required, which is why it works on devices as old as the iPhone 6S.
Which versions of Blender are supported?
BlendMotion supports Blender 4.0 and later. Older versions of Blender are not compatible due to API changes.
Can I use it over a USB cable instead of Wi-Fi?
Currently, BlendMotion connects over Wi-Fi using UDP. USB tethering is not supported at this time, but may be added in a future update.
Does it work with iPad?
Not yet ‚Äî the app is currently iPhone-only. iPad support is being considered for a future release.
Is there any latency?
On a local Wi-Fi network, latency is typically under 10ms. For the best experience, use a 5GHz Wi-Fi network and stay close to your router.
Does BlendMotion collect any data?
No. BlendMotion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All communication stays on your local network. See our Privacy Policy for details.
